üîê Encryption & Security

End-to-end encryption (E2EE)

Privora is designed so that message content is encrypted before it leaves your device and can only be decrypted by the intended recipients.

Important: Even with E2EE, some technical data (like routing and delivery metadata) may exist to operate the Service.

Encryption in transit

All network traffic between your app and our servers uses HTTPS/TLS.

Storage security

  • Access to production systems is restricted and monitored.
  • Passwords are stored using secure hashing.
  • We regularly update dependencies and apply security patches.

Biometrics

If you enable biometric features (e.g., app lock or biometric login), biometric data stays on your device. Privora does not receive or store your fingerprint/face data.
