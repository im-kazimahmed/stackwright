This Privacy Policy explains how Stackwright Studio (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use the CareerForge mobile application (the “App”) and related services.

Summary: CareerForge stores your account and career data (like resumes and job tracking entries) to provide the app’s functionality. We do not sell your personal information.

1) Data we collect

Depending on how you use the App, we may collect:

  • Account information: email address, password (stored securely as a hash), profile name (optional).
  • User content: resume content (sections like summary, experience, education, skills, projects, etc.) and job-tracking entries (company, role, notes, status).
  • Files you upload: attachments you choose to add to a job application (e.g., resume PDF, cover letter, offer letter).
  • AI feature usage: prompts/results history and credit usage (if you use AI tools in the App).
  • Diagnostics (optional): basic logs to troubleshoot errors (only if enabled in the App build).

2) How we use your information

  • Provide core features: resume building, exporting, job tracking, reminders, and attachments.
  • Authenticate you and secure your account.
  • Process exports (PDF/DOCX) and enable re-download from Export History.
  • Enable AI tools (rewrite bullets, ATS scoring, cover letters, etc.) and track credit usage.
  • Improve reliability, fix bugs, and prevent abuse.

3) Sharing of data

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data only when necessary to provide the service:

  • Service providers (e.g., hosting) that process data on our behalf under confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal requests if required to comply with applicable law or protect rights/safety.

If we integrate third-party analytics/ads in the future, we will update this policy and the Google Play Data Safety disclosures accordingly.

4) Security

  • We use industry-standard security practices to protect your data.
  • Data is intended to be transmitted over HTTPS (encrypted in transit) in production.
  • Passwords are stored as secure hashes, not in plain text.

5) Your choices & rights

  • Access & update: You can update your resume and job tracker information in the App.
  • Export: You can export resumes (PDF/DOCX) and re-download from Export History.
  • Delete: You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at contact.careerforge@gmail.com.

6) Data retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active to provide the service. If you request deletion, we will delete or anonymize your data unless we are required to keep certain information for legal or security reasons.

7) Children’s privacy

CareerForge is intended for users aged 13+ (recommended store targeting is 18+). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

8) Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version on this page and update the “Effective date”.
